Who We Are

A Foundation for Growth

TreeSproutHaven is a trauma-informed community organization dedicated to supporting at-risk individuals—particularly those who are marginalized by systemic barriers.

We specialize in providing holistic, culturally grounded care with a focus on out-of-detention and diversion, family reunification, and transitional housing programs. Through harm reduction, addiction recovery, mediation services, and Indigenous-led cultural healing initiatives, TreeSproutHaven fosters long-term recovery, reconnection, and reintegration into community life.

Our purpose is to create a resilient, culturally safe space for individuals recovering from trauma, addiction, incarceration, and displacement.

Our Services

Holistic Support

We offer a continuum of care designed to address the physical, emotional, and social needs of our community members.

Trauma-Informed Care

Prioritizing safety, trust, collaboration, empowerment, and cultural humility in every interaction.

Harm Reduction

Supporting individuals in reducing negative consequences of substance use while facilitating recovery options.

Family Reunification

Certified mediators support participants in repairing family relationships and navigating complex systems.

Addiction Recovery

Peer-led and clinical services, including group therapy, recovery circles, and wraparound case management.

Indigenous Culture

Centering Indigenous knowledge through elder guidance, land-based healing, and ceremonial supports.

Transitional Housing

Safe, stable housing with wraparound supports for employment, education, and long-term stability.
